The Eligibility criteria to Donate the Blood


  • The age is between 18 and 60.

  • The donor should not be suffering from transmitable disease. Like,

    • AIDS
    • HIV
    • Person having flew

  • The pulse rate is between 50 & 100 without irregularity

  • Haemoglobin level minimum of 12.5 gram/dL

  • The Pregnant women can' donate the blood as she required at that time.

  • A Time period between succesive blood donation should be more than 3 months.
